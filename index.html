About Drake Drum Studio

Hello! My name is Dario, and I'm an Italian drummer based in Ireland. I started my drumming journey at age 16, and I've been passionate about music and drumming ever since!

I studied at a drum academy in Sicily and continued learning as a self-taught drummer. Over the years, I've gained valuable experience playing live gigs and recording in home studios. Now, I'm excited to share my love for drumming with beginners in a fun, relaxed, and encouraging environment.

Whether you want to play your favorite songs, jam with friends, or simply explore rhythm and groove, I'll guide you step by step. Let's make music together!

What you will work on

Building strong foundations for your drumming journey

Timing & Rhythm

Master the foundation of all music. Learn to play with a metronome, understand different time signatures, and develop solid internal timing that keeps the band together.

Coordination

Train your hands and feet to work together. Build independence between limbs through exercises and patterns that become second nature with practice.

Groove & Feel

Learn what makes people move. Explore different styles from rock to funk, understanding how subtle changes create different grooves and musical feelings.

Confidence

Build the self-assurance to play with others. Learn to trust your skills, recover from mistakes, and express yourself freely through the drums.

Book a Trial Lesson

Ready to start your drumming journey? Book a 30-minute trial lesson to see if we're a good fit!

Beginner Basics

Perfect for absolute beginners

If you have never played drums before, this is where you start. We will cover all the fundamentals you need to feel comfortable behind the kit.

What you will learn:
  • Proper grip and stick technique
  • Basic posture and sitting position
  • Understanding the drum kit parts
  • Simple beats in 4/4 time
  • Reading basic drum notation
  • Practicing with a metronome

Duration: 30-45 minutes per lesson

Location: In person at my teaching studio

Groove & Timing

Develop solid rhythm and musical feel

Once you have the basics, it's time to work on your groove. This is what makes drumming musical and fun.

What you will learn:
  • Playing in time with a metronome
  • Exploring different music styles (rock, pop, funk, blues)
  • Building independence between hands and feet
  • Creating dynamic variation
  • Understanding subdivision and tempo
  • Developing your personal feel and touch

Duration: 30-45 minutes per lesson

Location: In person at my teaching studio

Play Along with Songs

Learn your favorite songs step by step

This is where drumming gets really exciting. We break down real songs and learn to play them from start to finish.

What you will learn:
  • How to listen and identify drum parts
  • Breaking down songs into sections
  • Learning fills, transitions, and accents
  • Building a repertoire of songs you love
  • Playing along with backing tracks
  • Preparing for jam sessions or bands

Duration: 30-45 minutes per lesson

Location: In person at my teaching studio
